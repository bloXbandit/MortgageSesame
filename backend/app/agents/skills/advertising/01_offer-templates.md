## OFFER CONSTRUCTION RULES

1. **Outcomes first.** Lead with what they GET, not what the product IS.
2. **Be specific.** "10 business days" beats "fast." "3.5% down" beats "low down payment."
3. **Address the objection inside the offer.** "Even if you've been declined before."
4. **Kenneth's mechanism is the proof.** Wholesale access + 10yr experience + DPA knowledge = unfair advantage.
5. **Never promise approval.** Never state a rate. Always soft CTA.
6. **"Why Kenneth" must be in every offer.** The offer doesn't just describe the product — it ties Kenneth's specific capability to the outcome.
